# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Opal Stones is for women seeking private bespoke fine jewellery: bridal pieces, meaningful gifts, heirloom redesign, and one-of-one commissions. They arrive with a personal occasion, a family stone, or an emotion they want translated into a jewel, and they expect discretion, taste, and direct atelier care.

## Product Purpose

The website presents Opal Stones as a private jewellery maison, not a retail store. Success means the visitor immediately feels the intimacy and precision of the atelier, understands that each piece is commissioned personally, and feels ready to request a private consultation.

## Brand Personality

Discreet, intimate, assured. The voice is editorial and restrained, with warmth carried through specific material language, private ritual, and careful pacing rather than generic luxury claims.

## Anti-references

Avoid jewellery store templates, catalogue grids, generic luxury websites, portfolio styling, AI-sounding copy, repeated logo placements, corporate language, crowded hero messaging, and overly decorative effects. Reference direction: Saint Laurent, The Row, Bottega Veneta, and Loro Piana for proportion, restraint, and quiet authority.

## Design Principles

1. Let the maison feel private before it feels commercial.
2. Use imagery as curation, not inventory.
3. Treat language as craft: short, exact, and emotionally controlled.
4. Give Arabic the same typographic authority as English.
5. Make every request path feel like a private appointment.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use WCAG AA as the baseline. Preserve bilingual Arabic and English support, connected Arabic typography, responsive layouts across desktop, tablet, and mobile, keyboard-operable controls, readable contrast, and reduced-motion alternatives for decorative movement.
